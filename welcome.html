If you want to try Archery, just come along to any of our beginner training sessions. We'll provide you with coaching and equipment. We even provide the few sessions free, so you can see if you like what we have to offer without any cost.

If you decide to join us, you will also recieve membership with the Scottish Archery Association, Archery GB, and the Scottish Field Archery Association if you so desire. You will also be provided with insurance so you can shoot with any club that is affiliated with the World Archery Federation.

We host or take part in several competitions, trips, and social events throughout the year. So whether you decide to join as a casual shooter or a competitive archer, we have something for almost everyone.


For your first sessions, we recommend not wearing baggy jumpers or tops and if you have long hair please have some way of tying it back too as these may catch on the string. We suggest flat shoes or trainers for footware too, definetely avoid high heels or open-toed shoes.

Find out more about Napier Archery


Edinburgh Napier University Archery Club mainly caters to students of Napier University. We run training sessions several times a week almost all year long. NUAC welcomes people of all experince levels, from competitive archers to people who have never even held a bow before. We even have a selection of club equipment that beginners can use to learn how to shoot with the help of our volunteer coaches.
